# FTIRproject
This project involves analysing Fourier Transform Infrared (FTIR) Spectroscopy data from Drosophila melanogaster

SexGenoFTIR.dat:
FTIR data of female and male samples across 12 mito-nuclear populations (AA1, AA2, AA3, AB1, AB2, AB3, BA1, BA2, BA3, BB1, BB2, and BB3)

AgeFTIR.dat:
FTIR data of female Dahomey flies aged 9 days old, 4 weeks old or 6 weeks old.

Diet1FTIR.dat:
FTIR data of female Dahomey flies previously fed on high lipid or SYA (control) diets for 1 week

Diet2FTIR.dat:
FTIR data of female Dahomey flies previously fed on diets containing 5%, 10%, or 20% yeast.

Diet-AgeFTIR.dat:
FTIR data of female Dahomey flies across 2 ages (9 days old or 3 weeks old) fed on diets containing 5%, 10% or 20% yeast.

DGRPFTIR.dat:
FTIR data of 108 DGRP populations

Diet2-fecundity.csv:
This excel sheet shows the number of eggs laid by Dahomey flies fed 5%, 10% or 20% yeast diets.
